## Key Terms

### Technical Terms

- **deep learning** - lowercase in prose
- **neural network** - two words, lowercase
- **backpropagation** - one word, lowercase
- **dataset** - one word, not "data set"
- **real-time** - hyphenated as adjective
- **state-of-the-art** - hyphenated when adjective
- **ground truth** - two words, no hyphen

### Dataset Names

- **ImageNet** - capital I and N
- **CIFAR-10** - all caps with hyphen
- **MNIST** - all caps

### Method Names

- **ResNet** - capital R and N (Residual Network)
- **VGGNet** - all caps VGG
- **AlexNet** - capital A and N

## Consistency Rules

### Numbers

- Spell out one through nine
- Use numerals for 10 and above
- Always numerals with units: 5 ms, 100 MB, 32 GB
